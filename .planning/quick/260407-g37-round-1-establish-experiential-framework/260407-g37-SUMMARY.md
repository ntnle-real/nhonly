## Pacing Interaction Model

**User Experience Flow:**
1. Load diorama: Fragment 0 "Nhơn Lý, 1976..." visible at center
2. User scrolls down 200px: Fragment 1 "The water holds the golden light..." appears (fragment 0 remains visible)
3. Continue scrolling: Each 200px reveals next fragment, text accumulates on screen
4. After scrolling 1400px total: Fragment 7 "He jumps..." becomes visible
5. Immediately, all text begins fading over 2 seconds
6. After fade complete: Only ocean canvas remains (no text)

**Design Intent Realized:**
- Witnessed, not narrated: Matter-of-fact tone, present-tense detail
- One element at a time: One sentence per scroll increment
- Continuous evolution: Text accumulates (previous lines stay visible)
- No jarring scene swaps: Smooth fade instead of hard disappearance
- Final state: Ocean alone (terminal fade after "He jumps...")
